    🏆 World Record
    Awareness Campaign

    Turmeric Ganesha Campaign — A Guinness & Asia Book of Records achievement

    Executed for the Karnataka State Pollution Control Board (KSPCB), this state-level awareness campaign reached over 1.58 crore citizens across Karnataka. More than 50,000 eco-friendly turmeric Ganesha idols were created, eliminating chemical pollution from water bodies. The campaign earned both the Asia Book of Records and the Guinness Book of Records — a landmark moment in eco-advocacy.

    Vanalok Field Team · Client: KSPCB & AYUSH Dept
    Nature Interpretation

    Bengal Fox Innovation & Incubation Centre, Salem — storytelling for a vulnerable species

    Built for the State Planning Commission, Tamil Nadu, this interpretation centre in Salem brings to life the little-known Bengal Fox — a species facing habitat loss and declining populations. Through immersive dioramas, interactive touchscreens, taxidermy, paw-print casts, and multilingual conservation panels, the centre now serves as a model for wildlife storytelling in India. Part of 16 such Nature Interpretation Centres established by Vanalok across 7 states.

    Vanalok Interpretation Team · Salem, Tamil Nadu
    Education & Outreach

    9 nature camps, 4 credit courses, and the classrooms that moved into the forest

    From Bandipur Tiger Reserve to university campuses, Vanalok has conducted 9 immersive nature camps, 4 university-level credit courses, and 8 specialised training programs reaching students, educators, forest officers, and communities. Partnering with WaterAid, FRLHT, JAIN University, UAS Dharwad & Shivamogga, and the Karnataka Forest Department, these programs transform how the next generation understands biodiversity — by living inside it.

    Academics & Outreach Wing · Across 7 States, 35+ Districts
