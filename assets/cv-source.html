01

Profile

A distinguished Indian Forest Service (IFS) officer with over two decades of leadership in forestry, wildlife management, environmental governance and public administration. Recognised for visionary initiatives integrating conservation, technology and sustainable development, he has led pioneering efforts from biodiversity conservation to the digital transformation of environmental governance. His tenures as Principal Secretary (Ecology & Environment), APCCF (Wildlife), Member Secretary (KSPCB) and Field Director (Kali Tiger Reserve) have been marked by innovation, impactful policy implementation, and award-winning eco-restoration and citizen-engagement programmes.
