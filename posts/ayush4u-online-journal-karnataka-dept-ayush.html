About Ayush4U

The Ayush4U online journal was established to serve as a platform for documenting research outcomes, clinical findings, and departmental initiatives in Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy across Karnataka. The journal aims to bridge traditional knowledge with modern documentation standards.

Karnataka's Ayush Footprint

Karnataka has a robust network of government Ayurveda hospitals, dispensaries, and research centres. The Government Ayurveda Research Centre in Mysuru — which publishes this journal — has been at the forefront of classical Ayurvedic research and classical formulation studies for decades.
